Air France operates a fleet of 216 aircraft. Its narrow-body fleet consists of all four Airbus A320 family variants, while its long-haul operations are served by the Boeing 777, 787, Airbus A330 and A350 XWB wide-body aircraft.[1] The airline has also ordered the short to medium-haul Airbus A220 to replace their aging A318s and A319s.[2]
