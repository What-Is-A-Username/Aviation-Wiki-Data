As of November 2020[update], Etihad Airways operates a fleet of both narrow body and widebody aircraft from four aircraft families Airbus A320ceo family, Airbus A380, Boeing 777 and Boeing 787 Series totaling 102 aircraft as of 10 November 2020.[1][2]
